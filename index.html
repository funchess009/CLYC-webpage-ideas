About Us

The Cold Lake Youth Council is a youth-led, volunteer-based organization that acts as peer advocates on civic and social issues. It is composed of youth ages 12-18 who live, work or attend school in Cold Lake. The CLYC represents a community investing in its youth, who are engaged and feel valued in our community. The CLYC was developed in March 2014, and has been running strong since. To join CLYC or attend a meeting as a guest, please contact Cold Lake and District FCSS at 780-594-4495 or email fcss@coldlake.com.
